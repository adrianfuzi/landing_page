features

Cube Cloud helps you store and share all your files and sensitive documents securely and anonymously in the cloud. You no longer have to worry about who can gain access to your sensitive data or what personal information leaks out.

end-to-end encryption

Our end-to-end encryption and security features prevents anyone from gaining insight into your private digital life. Your intellectual property is yours only, and it is kept safe and secure at all times.

zero knowledge

With zero knowledge encryption, you are in control of your privacy. Cube Cloud doesn't store your passwords or personal information, therefore it can't hand it out or leak it to anyone under any circumstances. Only you can access your data.

sync anything

Cube Cloud provides everything you need to access and share your data anywhere, anytime. Sync all your files and folders real-time and automatically across all your devices. Create download links to share large files or folders with your team.
